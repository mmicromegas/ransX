Quick Introduction

Interactive Web Studio for rans(eXtreme) is controlled by 3 dropdowns allowing you to select RANS equation you are interested in calculated from several 2D/3D simulations of core convection in stars at various resolutions done by the hydrodynamic code PROMPI.

Its operation is based on 4 simple steps:
  1. choose 2D/3D simulation from Model
  2. choose available resolution from Resolution
  3. choose RANS equation of interest from Equation
  4. click Submit
When you hover your mouse over the plots, pay attention to lower left corner for three symbols allowing you to zoom in as if you worked with python's matplotlib plots.

To obtain publication quality figures with better axis and legend labels, use original ransX.
